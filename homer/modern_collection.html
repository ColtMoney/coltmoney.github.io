Reviews & questions
How can you guarantee the lowest price on construction and still deliver the best quality?
Because we are a marketplace that partners with the best subcontractors, and construction teams. We give them consistent work, and do all the annoying things for them, so that we save them time, and get better prices for you. We created a new standard of vetting by going beyond the usual to deliver the highest quality work for you.
What if I want to change materials in the design?
You get to customise the design with our in-house experts. You’re always in control, and if you don’t love what we design with you, then you get your money back.
Do you make money on the materials?
No, because we have relationships with suppliers and buy in bulk we get discounted pricing. We then pass on the savings to you.
I found a less expensive construction team, can you match it?
Most times, yes. If we can’t for whatever we will work with the team you’ve found to get the job done. As long as they pass our quality testing standards.
How do you bring transparency to construction pricing?
Because we were sick & tired of getting quotes that ranged in tens of thousands of dollars difference, we built an algorithm that gets you an accurate quote, every time. That way we can guarantee to bring the highest quality construction, at the lowest price. We save money by doing everything remote & automated, instead of manually having someone spend hours in their day coming to your home, which they then bundle into the price along with every other quote they didn’t win.
How do you complete jobs faster, on cost & time?
Because we have everything ready to go for the construction team. Materials pre-ordered, professional drawings, etc. All they have to do is focus on what they’re good at, high quality work with everything ready for them instead of managing mind-numbing jobs.
