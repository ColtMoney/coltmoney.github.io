Thank you for stopping by!

We are Wedding Video Films. We design heart-crafted wedding films that move you… and are inspired by that one person who can make you feel like the rest of the world is standing still.

Our packages

Distinction

Your love is distinct, so your wedding film deserves to be too. Our cinematic artistry is a blend of your personal style, creative story shaping and heartfelt editing. We don’t follow formulas, and we only take on a limited number of weddings a year , far less than the average wedding video company. This ensures that we are always fresh, ready to roll and do not rush the post-production aspect of your film.

Every moment is carefully curated and every detail meticulously edited. We believe in quality-over-quantity and we want for you to re-experience your day with tears in your eyes.

Passion

There’s nothing we’re more passionate about than capturing how you express your passion for one another. It shows in our craft, and in every personalized step of our process.

Unfiltered

We’re pros at blending into your background and giving you room to be yourself. You won’t even know when we’re rolling, so you won’t feel pressured to put on a show. This is totally unobtrusive wedding film making. It is, after all a wedding for goodness sake and we don’t believe that the ‘bigger, better, bolder’ approach is needed. We think that in most cases it will distract from what should be the main focus -creating beautiful, wedding films.

Storytelling

We build feeling around your stories. Craft engaging narratives around your connections. And leave the pretty details in the background, where they belong. Because what matters most to us (and we hope you too) is capturing your genuine reactions and unscripted emotions as they unfold naturally. This is the heart of our art.
